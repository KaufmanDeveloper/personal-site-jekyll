About Me

I believe in constant improvement. At Knoxville Utilities Board, a concept my boss and mentor always reinforces is “Kaizen”, the Sino-Japanese word for “improvement”. If you can improve a little every day, the leaps you can make in a year are vast. Taking this approach to my work life has made every day exciting. Web development is a constantly changing and evolving field. It requires the willingness and desire to constantly be learning. This mindset has helped me learn Ember.js, Java REST and SOAP services, Peoplecode, Oracle and Microsoft SQL, Git, Node.js, Jekyll, and continuous integration practices.

One of the other principles I try to live by is ABC: Always Be Coding. On the weekends or after work I’ll dedicate time to work on my personal projects. Whether it be a side web application to help teachers or a video game project, I always want to be exploring new concepts and learning. It’s also resulted in me picking up an interest in music, especially in modular synthesis. My soundcloud has a few of these tracks.
